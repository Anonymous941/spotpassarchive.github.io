SpotPass Archival Project

As you may know, online services for the Nintendo 3DS and 2DS and Wii U family of systems are shutting down in April: Link to official announcement

All online communication services, including the distribution of SpotPass data will be discontinued on April 8th, 2024, at 4pm PDT.

As a result, features using SpotPass in many titles will stop working once this change has taken effect.

Popular 3DS titles using SpotPass features include:

  • StreetPass Mii Plaza: SpotPass was used to distribute special Miis (often of Nintendo employees) and puzzle pieces for the Puzzle Swap minigame. Without SpotPass, Puzzle Swap would normally not receive new puzzles.
  • Mario Kart 7: SpotPass was used to receive a message enabling the use of the DLC. Without SpotPass, the DLC would become unusable.

3DS/2DS and Wii U systems store a local database of titles for which you have recently received SpotPass data. The database can be used to check for the URLs it uses to download the SpotPass content.

By dumping this database from your system(s), you can help Pretendo in preserving SpotPass content, ensuring that SpotPass features in these titles can be used long after Nintendo has pulled the plug on the service.

Nintendo 2DS/3DS
